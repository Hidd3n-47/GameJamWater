How To Play?
Press ESC for Pause Menu.
Use SPACEBAR to toggle between Map and Puzzle view. 
Prevent the cities from reaching 0% by completing the puzzle that has the same colour as the city (sticky note). 
Your goal is to reach Day 3 (Dec 3).

Failure States: 
- Completing a puzzle when not needed (deducts 25%)
- Letting a city hit 0% (restarts game)

Win State:
- Reach and complete Day 3
